Salmon And Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make this best salmon and rice in the most simple way possible! It is quick, easy, and delicous!

Ingredients

  • Wild caught salmon
  • White rice
  • Lime
  • Olive oil
  • Spices: Salt, Pepper, Onion powder, Garlic Powder, Paprika.

Steps

  1. Put one cup of rice and two cups of water in rice maker and cook
  2. Wash and dry salmon
  3. Coat salmon generously with high quality olive oil
  4. Salt just the skin side of the salmon
  5. Squeeze lime juice over the meat side of the salmon and season with all seasonings listed
  6. Place salmon in airfryer and set on 400 degrees for 12 minutes
  7. Once rice is done put it in a bowl and squeeze lime juice on the rice and season with salt and pepper
  8. Finally, put it all together and there you go! quick easy and delicious salmon and rice!
